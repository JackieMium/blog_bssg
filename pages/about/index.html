About this blog

Why another? It is not like I write a lot. Well to be honest I guess I always intend to read, write and post more. But the ugly truth is, it has became kind of my habit to draft posts from time to time, though few of them got eventually finished. That said, I am toying around with this very interesting blog generator that is powered by Bash. Firing bash commands, writing in my beloved Vim editor, and (automatically) converting markdown files to html files for publishing, the whole process is simple, elegant but nevertheless intuiging to me. It would also occurred to me as friendly if I ever want to write and post on other platforms, be it BSD, Windows (oh god I hope I won't [have to] do this often) or even Android.

So to answer the question, why another?

Why not if I can and I want.
